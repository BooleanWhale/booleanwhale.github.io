About Me

Hi, my name is Ashley Saleem-West. I'm a designer based St Albans, a wonderful town near London in the UK. I describe myself as a passionately user-focused designer who loves designing, dabbling in code, and the web platform.

I started as a graphic designer and becoming acquainted with product development through personal E-Commerce projects before moving onto E-Service Europe Ltd, to redesign and develop their E-Commerce website.

With experience in Figma and Adobe software, I am always pushing myself to learn more and have been making use of the time saved from the extra spare time in 2020/2012 to learn more about UX design and web development.

I think of performance and design as two sides of the same coin, which is why I push for great user experience without a heavy overhead. I like to ask myself how I can make an application easy, enjoyable, and fast to use.

When I'm not designing, I can be found drawing or caring for my two adorable clownfish.
